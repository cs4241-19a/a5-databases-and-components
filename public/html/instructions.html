HOW TO PLAY



As you wait, you will notice that orders will start to line up in front of the ice cream truck. These are the orders that need to be fulfilled in order to make money!



In order to complete the orders and earn money, you need to press on the key that corresponds to the correct menu item! The keys are 1, 2, 3, and 4 for the corresponding flavors. You will earn the amount that the menu item costs.


Be careful not to press the wrong button for any of the orders; you will lose $10 no matter what flavor you messed up! You lose when you mess up 5 times or if you have more than 15 backed up orders!




Warning: There is sound in this game!
